Vlădeasa

30 km Adventure

The location is around 1 h and 30 minutes away from Cluj-Napoca. The trail usually starts at the Vladeasa Cabin, up until where you can drive with your car, but us, for some extra km, we started from the village down below called Rogojel. The trail itself is supposedly 13 km long, but we picked by mistake another route that ended up making us walk 30 km from Rogojel, up to the top and back to the car in the village.
Even if is a pretty long trail, the beautiful views and the multitude of breath taking spots are worth the hussel.

The trail itself is not difficult and I would place it somewhere similar with the Râmetea one, here taking in consideration the lenght. The route is pretty clean and without any major difficult portion and can be enjoyed with the family. If the other hikers are right the lenght itself should be around 13 km, but if you happen to try to go all around as we did, through a car forest road that we took by mistake, you may end up with 30 km at the end.

Vladeasa Peak stands at an altitude of 1836 m. Taking in consideration how high it is depending on the season you decide to hike be prepared for weather situations. We hiked in autumn, in October. The weather was pretty decent down, but up on top it reached -3 with sharp winds that made it feel at least as -5/-6. The entire trial provides various locations and elements for photography, from superb mushrooms, splendid forests, bird eye view above the hills any many more! So get dressed, pack you bags and get your cameras ready! Enjoy a weekend hike to Vladeasa Peak and why not, maybe a photoshoot with us as well!
