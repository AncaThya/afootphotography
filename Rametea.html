Râmetea

How Little We Are

Our trail starts from the beautiful village of Râmetea that is 1 h away from Cluj-Napoca. It is amazing for a trip with friends and family and exceptionally beatiful for bird eye view pictures. The ideal location for a fabulous photoshoot above the world below.

The whole trail took us around 5-6 h from the village up to the "Piatra Secuiului" Peak and back. I need to mention that we took a long break on top where we relaxed we ate and took a number of pictures and explored the steep edges. The hike itself could be around 2h going up and 2 h going back. In my opinion is a easy to medium difficulty, taking in consideration the steepness of the trail and the rocky road.

There are multiple trails that start from Râmetea and go around and above the village. Our trail could have been extended to a circuit that could have taken a couple more hours. The region is beautiful an worth a try. Splendid for a weekend adventure and for amazing pictures!
