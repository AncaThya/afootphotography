Small Adventure

Close to the beautiful village called Marisel, located in a mountain area at about 1 h away from Cluj-Napoca, we can find this stunning spot that provides a splendid view over the vallies in the area.  A location ideal for a family photoshoot.

The trail itself is extremly easy and the scenaries and the areas are amazing. The road can be done by anyone and takes around 20-25 minutes. If you decide to come to this place you should also check other beautiful locations around there.

You can go and see the Belis Lake and also the stunning view from Marisel. It's a fabulous location to visit, especially in winter, as Marisel is well known for its ski areas and mountain cabins. Highly recommended for a family trip in the weekend where you can also make a picnic and take beautiful shots.
